This poem is called 'I Am Caribbean'. I chose this poem because it was a fun childrens Caribbean poem.

I Am Caribbean

I am from the Caribbean -

Where the sun is hot,

But the breeze is cool.

I am from the Caribbean -

Where flowers are bright,

And so are the kites.

I am from the Caribbean -

Where the sand is white,

But sometimes black.

I am from the Caribbean -

Where a girl is a gyal,

And a boy is a bwoi.

I am from the Caribbean -

Where the islands are many,

But the people are one.


-Eva Greene Wilson
