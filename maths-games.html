24 Game

How it works

How to set it up

First, you get an ordinary pack of cards.Then you select 4 of them, and lay them out on the table.

How to play

Aim: To make 24 out of the 4 cards

Rules of the game:

  1. You can only use each number once.
  2. You can use addition, subtraction, multiplacation & division.
  3. You can use as many sums as you want.
  4. You can any amount of arithmetic operations as you want, say you could use an addition sum and a subtraction sum.
  5. Use your brain and have fun!
